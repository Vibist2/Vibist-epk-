About Vibist

Vibist is an emerging Afrobeat artist whose music blends emotional storytelling, relatable lyrics, and infectious melodies. Inspired by real-life experiences, love, ambition, pain, and hope, Vibist creates songs that connect with listeners on a personal level.


Growing up with big dreams and a passion for music, Vibist developed a unique style that combines the rhythm of Afrobeat with the soul and emotion of R&B. His songwriting focuses on telling authentic stories, giving listeners music they can feel long after the song ends.
